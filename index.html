Zhipeng Bao

Hey, my name is Zhipeng Bao, currently a third-year PhD student at Robotics Institute, Carnegie Mellon University supervised by Prof. Martial Hebert. I also earned my master degree (MSR) at CMU supervised by Prof. Martial Hebert and Prof. Yu-Xiong Wang. I recieved my bachelor degree majoring in Electronic Engineering from Tsinghua University. During my undergraduation, I also worked with Prof. Shaodi You from ANU and Prof. Min Xu from CMU.

My research interest lies in the interaction between generative modeling and self-supervised object-centric representation learning.

I love foods, traveling, and taking photos.
